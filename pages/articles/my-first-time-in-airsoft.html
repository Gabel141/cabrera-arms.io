My First Time in Airsoft (w.i.p)

by Gabel Cabrera, May 4th 2025

My first time in airsoft was an exhilerating experience, packed with action and excitement it felt so incredibly immersive. I had long dreamed of being able to play airsoft, growing up around video games, movies, tv shows, and cartoons that so oftened featured firearms. I wanted to be able experience that kind of action, even if I wasn't shooting the real thing.

I was lucky in that my brothers already had gear ready for me, so that I could experience it alongside them and allow them to give me more experienced advice.

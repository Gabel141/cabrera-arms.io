CABRERA ARMS

Cabrera Arms is an independent website created by Gabel Ethan Cabrera to provide a resource into learning about both firearms and airsoft replicas. The website aims to become a credible source of information regarding a weapon's history, functionality, use, parts, and other relevant details.

GOALS

To serve as a credible and reliable source for firearms and airsoft replicas. To provide specific details and fun facts about the weapon presented, as well as their appearances in video games.
